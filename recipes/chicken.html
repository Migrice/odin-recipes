Chicken

Description

The chicken is very good. When well cooked, you can eat it during ceremonies like weedings, birthdays, etc.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • oignon
  • bell pepper
  • ail
  • Djansan
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken in small pieces after cleaning it
  2. Cut oignon, bell pepper
  3. Crush ail, djansan
  4. Put all in the pot, add salt and water
